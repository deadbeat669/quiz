Here is a timeline of his life

  • Boba Fett was created from the genetic code of Jango Fett, a human bounty hunter who was used as the blueprint for the clone army. Unlike the other clones Boba is raised by Jango as his son.
  • Boba and Jango were hired a Twi'lek to capture his daughter and bring her back alive. As a child Boba kills to other bounty hunters to complete his mission.
  • Jango and Boba are sent to kill a Senator.
  • Kenobi discovers the Fetts on the cloner planet. Kenobi chases the Fetts when they flee.
  • Boba witnesses the death of Jango on the planet Geonosis by a Jedi.
  • Still a child Boba joins a group of bounty hunters and tries to get his revange on Master Windu, the Jedi that killed Jango.
  • Bobba hunts down Han Solo and captures him to bring back to Jabba
  • Bobba is knocked into the sarlacc pit by Luke.
